YouTube is a video sharing website. This website allows for users to upload content, like videos, share videos, and view videos. Youtube contains a variety of content, like movies, gaming, tutorials, and music. Also, users are able to subscribe to channels, like or dislike videos, and comment on them.

YouTube is used to view and share content. Youtube is a website made for entertainment and also educational purposes. Youtube is used for live streaming, sharing news, and shows anything a person searches up. This website can also be used as a job when content is being created and uploaded by a person.
